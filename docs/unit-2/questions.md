## Descriptive Questions

<details>
<summary>1. Describe the Disaster Management Cycle and briefly explain the activities involved in Pre-disaster, During-disaster, and Post-disaster phases. (click to see key points)</summary>
<details>
<summary>Click to see Key Points</summary>
<strong>Key Points:</strong><br>
- <strong>Cycle:</strong> A continuous process.<br>
- <strong>Pre-disaster:</strong> Prevention, Mitigation, Preparedness (Risk assessment, Early Warning).<br>
- <strong>During-disaster:</strong> Response (Search & Rescue, First Aid, Relief).<br>
- <strong>Post-disaster:</strong> Recovery, Rehabilitation, Reconstruction (Build Back Better).
</details>
</details>

<details>
<summary>2. Discuss the role of the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) and Odisha Disaster Rapid Action Force (ODRAF) in disaster management. (click to see key points)</summary>
<details>
<summary>Click to see Key Points</summary>
<strong>Key Points:</strong><br>
- <strong>NDRF:</strong> Specialized national force, highly trained for diverse disasters (CBRN, floods, collapse), rapid deployment.<br>
- <strong>ODRAF:</strong> Specialized state force (Odisha), focus on cyclones/floods, road clearance, immediate rescue.<br>
- <strong>ODRAF:</strong> Specialized state force (Odisha), focus on cyclones/floods, road clearance, immediate rescue.<br>
- <strong>Coordination:</strong> Both work in coordination with local authorities and communities.
</details>
