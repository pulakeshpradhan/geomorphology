# Role of Stakeholders in Disaster Management

Disaster management is a multi-disciplinary effort.

![Institutional Framework](../assets/images/tm_institutional_framework_india.png)
*Fig 1: Institutional Framework for Disaster Management in India.*

## 1. National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA)

* **Official Website:** [ndma.gov.in](https://ndma.gov.in/)
* **Role:** Apex body headed by the Prime Minister. Policies, plans, and guidelines.
* **Mandate:** DM Act 2005.

## 2. National Institute of Disaster Management (NIDM)

* **Official Website:** [nidm.gov.in](https://nidm.gov.in/)
* **Role:** Capacity building, training, research, and documentation.
* **Publications:** Produces training modules and case studies.

## 3. National Disaster Response Force (NDRF)

* **Official Website:** [ndrf.gov.in](https://www.ndrf.gov.in/)
* **Role:** Specialized force for specialized response.
* **Motto:** "Aapda Seva Sadaiv Sarvatra" (Disaster Service Always Everywhere).

## 4. Odisha State Disaster Management Authority (OSDMA)

* **Official Website:** [osdma.org](https://www.osdma.org/)
* **Role:** The first dedicated disaster management authority in India (est. 1999).
* **Achievements:** Received the **Subhash Chandra Bose Aapda Prabandhan Puraskar** for excellence in disaster management.

## 5. Odisha Disaster Rapid Action Force (ODRAF)

* **Role:** Specialized state force for search and rescue.
* **Structure:** Units are stationed across the state for rapid deployment.

## 6. NGOs and International Bodies

* **UNDRR:** [undrr.org](https://www.undrr.org/) - Global policy and framework (Sendai).
* **Red Cross:** [icrc.org](https://www.icrc.org/en) - Humanitarian aid.
  * *Article:* [Role of NGOs in Disaster Management](https://nidm.gov.in/PDF/pubs/India%20Disaster%20Report%202013.pdf)

### Descriptive Questions

1. **Discuss the role of the National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA).**
    <details>
    <summary>Click to see Key Points</summary>
    *   **Apex Body:** Headed by the PM.
    *   **Policy:** Lays down policies, plans, and guidelines for DM.
    *   **Coordination:** Ensures enforcement and implementation of the DM Act.
    *   **Funding:** Recommends provision of funds for mitigation.
    </details>

2. **Briefly explain the significance of the Odisha State Disaster Management Authority (OSDMA).**
    <details>
    <summary>Click to see Key Points</summary>
    *   **Pioneer:** First dedicated SDMA in India (set up after the 1999 Super Cyclone).
    *   **Success:** Transformed Odisha from a disaster-vulnerable state to a model of resilience (e.g., Phailin evacuation).
    *   **Innovation:** Built multipurpose cyclone shelters and set up ODRAF and SATARK.
    </details>
